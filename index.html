Welcome to the Wool Croft

Beautiful Yarns, Crafts & Handmade Gifts

The Wool Croft is a wool shop in the heart of Abergavenny, Wales. All your favourite quality hand knitting yarns are here, plus beautiful Welsh wool, embroidery, felting, cross stitch, fabrics, haberdashery and trims, kids crafts, handmade gifts and more.

About Us:
Quality yarns and wools

We stock the following quality brands and more: Rowan Yarns (we are a Rowan Flagship store with the whole range), West Yorkshire Spinners, Noro, , Olympus Sashiko threads, Hawthorn Handmade kits and handmade gifts by other independent local makers.

We are also an agent for Baavet British wool duvets.
